Lasagna

Description

Delicious layers of lasagna noodles, sauce, cheese, and ground beef - baked to perfection!

Make a weeknight dinner a breeze by preparing the night before!

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • pasta sauce
  • Italian blend shredded cheese
  • ground beef
  • garlic salt
  • parsley
  • non-stick cooking spray

    • Steps

    1. Prepare lasagna noodles according to package.
    2. Brown ground beef in a frying pan on the stove.
    3. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
    4. Spray baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
    5. Pour pasta sauce in the baking dish - enough to lightly cover the bottom.
    6. Line the bottom of the baking dish with lasagna noodles, creating a single layer.
    7. Spread a layer of pasta sauce over the noodles.
    8. Sprinkle approximately 1/4 of the ground beef over the noodle and sauce layers.
    9. Cover your current layers with Italian cheese blend.
    10. Repeat layering steps until baking dish is nearly full. You can omit ground beef for the top-most layer.
    11. Sprinkel garlic salt on top layer to taste.
    12. Garnish with parsley.
    13. Bake, uncovered, for at least 40 minutes. If you prefer your cheese to be slightly browned, you can bake longer.
