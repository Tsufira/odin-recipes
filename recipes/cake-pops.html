Cake Pops

Description

Perfectly sweet mouthfuls of moist cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 package yellow cake mix
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 8oz cream cheese, softened
  • lollipop sticks
  • 14oz chocolate confectioner's coating

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Grease 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Using an electric mixer, beat cake mix, water, eggs, and vegetable oil on low for 30 seconds, then increase speed to medium for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour prepared batter into greased baking dish.
  5. Bake in pre-heated over until toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clear - approximately 28-33 minutes.
  6. Allow cake to cool completely - at least 1 hour.
  7. Crumcle caek into a large bowl.
  8. Mix cream cheese with cake crumbles.
  9. Roll cake mixture into quarter-sized balls - about 40-50.
  10. Insert a lollipop stick into each cake call and arrange on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  11. Refrigerate for atleast 30 minutes.
  12. Melt confectioner's chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl in 15-second intervals, stirring between each, until completely melted - 1 to 3 minutes total.
  13. Dip each chilled cake call into the melted chocolate until coated, placing them back on the baking sheet to set..
