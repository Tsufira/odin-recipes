Zuppa Toscana

Description

Warm, hearty soup with salty and sweet tones. The perfectly delicious soup to warm you up on a cold day.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground mild Italian sausage
  • 1 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 4 slices bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp minces garlic
  • 5 (13.75oz) cans chicken broth
  • 6 potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 bunch fresh kale, tough stems removed and chopped

Steps

  1. Brown Italian sausage and red pepper flakes in Dutch over on stove until sausge is no longer pink, rougly 10 to 15 mintues. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook bacon in the same Dutch oven over medium heat until crisp. Drain, leaving a few tablespoons of drippings with the bacon in the bottom of the Dutch oven.
  3. Add onions and garlic to Dutch oven. Cook until onions are soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour chicken broth into Dutch oven. Bring to a boil.
  5. Add potatoes and boil until tender, about 20 mintues.
  6. Reduce to medium heat and stir in heavy cream and cooked sausage. Heat thoroughly.
  7. Mix kale into soup just before serving.
